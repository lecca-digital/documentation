## Key Features

- **Variety of Integration Types**: Supports API, OAuth2, Basic (username/password), and Key Pair (private/public key) integrations.
- **Easy Setup**: Enter a connection name, an optional description, and proceed with specific integration instructions.
- **Security**: Connection data is encrypted on our backend, ensuring secure transmission and decryption takes place server-side during requests.
- **Flexibility**: Establish connections at a workspace level or project level to suit your needs.

## Creating a Connection

<Steps>

### Step 1: Initiate Connection

- Navigate to the Connections section in your Lecca.io dashboard.
- Click on "New Connection" to start the setup process.

### Step 2: Enter Connection Details

- **Name**: Give your connection a memorable name.
- **Description**: Optionally, add a description to help identify the connection's purpose.

### Step 3: Select Integration Type

- Choose the type of integration from the options:
  - **API Integration**: Fill out the API endpoint details and authentication headers.
  - **OAuth2 Integration**: A link will be provided for authentication with the third-party app.
  - **Basic Authentication**: Enter your username and password credentials.
  - **Key Pair Integration**: Provide the necessary private and public keys.

### Step 4: Finalize and Save

- Once all required fields are filled, save your connection.
- Your connection is now ready to enhance your workflow or AI project.

</Steps>
