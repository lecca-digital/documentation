# Workspaces

**Workspaces in Lecca.io are where you organize your projects, collaborate with your team, and manage your automations and agents.**

## Creating a new Workspace

Setting up a workspace in Lecca.io helps you organize your projects and manage your resources efficiently. Follow these steps to create and configure your workspace.

<Callout type="info">
  When you create an account, you will automatically have a personal workspace
  created for you.
</Callout>

<Steps>
### Step 1: Create a new Workspace

1. At the top of your screen, click on your Workspace to display the workspace selection menu.
2. Select **New Workspace** from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter a name for your workspace and provide a brief description if needed.
4. Click **Create** to create the workspace.

### Step 2: Complete onboarding

1. Our onboarding wizard will guide you through the process of setting up your new workspace.

</Steps>

## Managing your Workspace

Only Maintainers can manage workspaces. As a Maintainer, you can invite team members, manage billing, and more.

## Deleting your Workspace

You can delete an additional workspace by going to your workspace settings, after you have cancelled your subscription. You can cancel your subscription by going to your workspace billing settings and clicking on the link **Manage payment methods**.

<Callout>
  You cannot delete your default created workspace (The workspace created when
  you first made your account).
</Callout>
