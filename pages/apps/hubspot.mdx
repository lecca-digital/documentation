# HubSpot Integration

HubSpot is a cloud-based platform that connects marketing, sales, and customer service tools into a single CRM database. This integration allows you to automate various actions related to contacts within your HubSpot account.

## Actions

### Upsert Contact

This action allows you to create a new contact or update an existing contact in your HubSpot CRM. It ensures that your contact information is always up-to-date.

### Create Contact

Use this action to add a new contact to your HubSpot CRM. This is useful for capturing new leads or customer information.

### Update Contact

This action updates the details of an existing contact in your HubSpot CRM. It helps in maintaining accurate and current contact records.

### Add Contact to List

With this action, you can add a contact to a specific list within HubSpot. This is useful for segmenting your contacts for targeted marketing campaigns.

### Remove Contact from List

This action allows you to remove a contact from a specific list in HubSpot. It helps in managing your contact lists effectively.

## Triggers

There are no triggers available for the HubSpot integration at this time.

## Connections

HubSpot integration uses OAuth2 for authentication. This ensures secure and seamless connectivity between Lecca.io and your HubSpot account.
