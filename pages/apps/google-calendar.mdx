# Google Calendar Integration

Google Calendar is a time-management and scheduling calendar service developed by Google. By integrating Google Calendar with Lecca.io, you can automate and manage your calendar events efficiently.

## Actions

### Create Event

Create a new event in your Google Calendar. Specify the details such as event title, description, start time, end time, and location to organize your schedule.

### Update Event

Modify an existing event in your Google Calendar. Update details like the event title, time, location, and description as necessary.

### Add Guests to Event

Invite guests to an existing calendar event. Manage your attendee list to enhance collaboration and coordination.

### List Calendars

Retrieve a list of calendars associated with your Google account. This allows you to manage and select specific calendars for your events.

### List Events

Fetch events from your Google Calendar. View details of upcoming, past, or specific events based on your needs.

## Triggers

### New Calendar Event

Be notified whenever a new event is created in your Google Calendar. Utilize this trigger to automate responses or workflows upon event creation.

## Connections

### OAuth2

Authenticate your Google Calendar account securely using OAuth2. This connection ensures that your calendar data is accessible for automation while maintaining privacy and security.
