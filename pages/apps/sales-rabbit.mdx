# SalesRabbit Integration

SalesRabbit is the leading field sales management solution, designed to optimize and streamline the sales process for field teams. By integrating SalesRabbit with Lecca.io, you can automate various aspects of your sales operations.

## Actions

### List Lead Status Activities

Retrieve a list of lead status activities from SalesRabbit. This action allows you to monitor and analyze the status changes of your leads.

## Triggers

### Lead Status Updated on Device

This trigger activates when a lead's status is updated on a device within SalesRabbit. It enables real-time updates and actions based on changes in lead status ensuring your team is always informed and responsive.

## Connections

SalesRabbit requires an API key for establishing a connection. Make sure to have your API key ready to authenticate and connect SalesRabbit with Lecca.io.
