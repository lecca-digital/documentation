# Microsoft Outlook Integration

Microsoft Outlook is primarily an email and calendar application that's available as part of Microsoft's Office 365 suite. This integration allows you to automate tasks related to emails and calendar events within Outlook.

## Actions

### Create Draft

Create a new email draft in your Microsoft Outlook account. This action allows you to prepare emails that can be sent later.

### Send Draft

Send an existing email draft from your Microsoft Outlook account. This action finalizes and sends the draft email to the intended recipients.

## Triggers

### Email Received

Trigger workflows when a new email is received in your Microsoft Outlook inbox. This allows you to automate responses or actions based on incoming emails.

## Connections

To connect Microsoft Outlook with Lecca.io, you will need to use OAuth2 authentication. This ensures secure access to your Outlook account and allows the integration to perform actions and triggers on your behalf.
