# Google Forms Integration

Google Forms allows you to create online forms and surveys with multiple question types. This integration enables you to connect Google Forms with Lecca.io to automate your workflows.

## Actions

There are no actions available for the Google Forms integration at this time.

## Triggers

### New Form Response

Trigger workflows in Lecca.io when a new response is submitted to a Google Form. This allows you to automate tasks based on the data collected from your forms.

## Connections

Google Forms uses OAuth2 for authentication. Ensure you have the necessary permissions to connect your Google Forms account with Lecca.io.
