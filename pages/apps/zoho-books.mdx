# Zoho Books Integration

Zoho Books is a cloud-based accounting software that is set to take your business a step further into the future. This integration allows you to connect your Zoho Books account with Lecca.io to streamline your accounting processes.

## Actions

There are currently no actions available for the Zoho Books integration.

## Triggers

There are currently no triggers available for the Zoho Books integration.

## Connections

### OAuth2 Connection

To connect your Zoho Books account to Lecca.io, you will need to use OAuth2 authentication. This connection is specifically configured for the US servers (.com).
