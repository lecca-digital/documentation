YouTube is an online video sharing platform owned by Google. This integration allows you to connect your YouTube account with Lecca.io to enhance your workflows and AI Agents.

The YouTube integration uses OAuth2 for secure authentication and connection. This ensures that your data and interactions with YouTube are protected and authorized.
